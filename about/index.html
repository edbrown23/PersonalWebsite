About Me!

Hello everyone! My name is Eric Brown, and I'm a Software Engineer at Aereo Inc. I love Computer Science, but that's only a cursory glance at everything I enjoy. If you'd like to know more, read on!

Professionally speaking, I would describe myself as a highly passionate software developer who wants nothing more for the future than to work with other passionate engineers and designers who live and breathe code as I do. I constantly seek new ways to challenge myself, and keep a long backlog of skills to learn and projects to complete, far beyond the requirements of my classes. I have experience developing for embedded systems, primarily in C on Atmel microcontrollers, and for desktop applications in a wide variety of languages including C, C++, Java, Python, and more. I consider myself obsessed with code beauty and optimization, though I am aware that progress is ultimately most important.

Personally, I'm a budding audiophile, technology lover, and amatuer sailor. I enjoy passionate debate, on any issue, and love the idea of cities, especially since I live in a rather small one. If you'd like to contact me, read my blog, get information on my various projects, or check out my resume check out the links at the top of the page.
